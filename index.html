Bushfires in Australia in 2020

The 2019-20 Australian bushfires were catastrophic in scale and impact - both for people and for nature. Up to 19 million hectares were burnt, with 12.6 million hectares primarily forests and woodlands.

Here we can see that majority of the area that was impacted were forests and the state most affected was NSW

From this heatmap, we can tell that devastatingly a majority of the area burnt in NSW were part of the nature conservation.
